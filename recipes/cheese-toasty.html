Cheese Toasty

Description

5 minute winner!

    Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Butter
  • CHeese of your choice
    Steps
  1. Butter the bread and slice the cheese
  2. Build the sandwich - butter facing out
  3. Place on a hot frying pan
  4. Flip after a few minutes
  5. Serve with homemade chilli ketchup
