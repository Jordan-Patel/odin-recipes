Vegetable Curry

Description

A vegan twist on a classic Italian staple

Nothing will fill a hungry tummy better!

    Ingredients
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tin of tomatoes
  • 3 pieces of veg of your choice
  • Spice mix
  • Stock
  • Garlic
  • Vegan cheese and sour cream
  • Lasagna Sheets
    Steps
  1. Chop Onion and Vegetables

  3. After a few minutes, add the spice mix

  5. Add the chopped tomotoes and bring to a boil. Then lower to a simmer.
  6. Add the stock
  7. Serve with fresh coriander and naan bread
