Vegetable Curry

Description

This is one of my favorite dishes and can be knocked up in 30 mins.

Full flavor, simple ingredients and spices that'll knock your socks off!

    Ingredients
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tin of tomatoes
  • 3 pieces of veg of your choice
  • Spice mix
  • Stock
    Steps
  1. Chop Onion and Vegetables
  2. Add the chopped onion to a hot pan
  3. After a few minutes, add the spice mix
  4. Add the Vegetables and fry for 5 minutes
  5. Add the chopped tomotoes and bring to a boil. Then lower to a simmer.
  6. Add the stock
  7. Serve with fresh coriander and naan bread
